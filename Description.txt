## Description:

​	Neor_mini is a mobile base with an Ackerman steering structure, equipped with ROS operating system, which can realize 2D and 3D mapping and navigation demonstration functions. This document will teach you how to start from the perspective of simulation and real vehicles. Started to build an Ackerman ROS car. The content involved: URDF ROS Ackerman odometer drive, mapping, navigation demonstration, camera patrol, multi-ultrasonic obstacle avoidance, VLP 16-line radar 3D mapping, Examples of camera calibration, Raspberry Pi monocular recognition and range, etc.Also includes ROS entry and advanced tutorials based on the Neo_mini physical car.
